Blockoid

Blockoid is a 2D sandbox game in development. Superficially, the game is similar to titles such as Terraria or Minecraft, but the aim of Blockoid is to provide a more dynamic world for the player, where danger and adventure do not sit statically in waiting for you! Instead, you may encounter monsters and adventurers roaming the world with their own goals, just as you do!

This game is written entirely in pure Java. All art assets were drawn by myself.

Current Features:
  • Procedurally generated world with various biomes
  • Dynamic lighting, dynamic environment
  • Basic inventory and items
  • Basic NPCs and AI
  • Basic combat system and physics
Planned Features:
  • Crafting system
  • Improved AI
  • Additional Content
  • Multiplayer
